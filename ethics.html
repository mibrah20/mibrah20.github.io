IT Ethics

Ethical considerations in the IT field is important for maintaining trust with everyone. Technology allows users to experience a lot of benefits, but it can also cause harmful outcomes as well. Here are some ethical issues that are affecting IT in today's society:

  • Misinformation and Deepfake - Privacy Invasion and misuse of identity is high, resulting in personal information being taken advantage of in the wrong manner .
  • Lack of general Oversight - A lot of companies hire third party tech to oversee important information and data, but confusion as who is resposible for certain issues can come about.
  • Significant use of Artificial Intelligence - Facial recognition software can be misused resulting in jobs being replaced or even racial bias occuring.
