IT Ethics

Ethical considerations in IT are important for building trust with others. Technology offers many benefits, but it can also lead to harmful effects. Here are some ethical issues impacting IT today.

  • Widespread use of Artificial Intelligence - Facial recognition software can be misused, leading to job displacement or even racial bias.
  • Lack of oversight - Companies often use third-party tech to manage important data, but this can lead to confusion about who is responsible for specific issues.
  • Misinformation and Deepfakes - They invade privacy and misuse identities, often leading to the abuse of personal information.
