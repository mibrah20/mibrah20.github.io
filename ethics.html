IT Ethics

Ethical considerations in the IT field are important for maintaining trust with everyone. Technology allows users to experience a lot of benefits, but it can also cause harmful outcomes as well. Here are some ethical issues that are affecting IT in today's society:

  • Misinformation and Deepfake - Privacy invasion and misuse of identity are high, resulting in personal information being taken advantage of in the wrong manner.
  • Lack of general oversight - Many companies hire third-party tech to oversee important information and data, but confusion about who is responsible for certain issues can arise.
  • Significant use of Artificial Intelligence - Facial recognition software can be misused, resulting in jobs being replaced or even racial bias occurring.
